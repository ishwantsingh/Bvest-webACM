About

BVEST 2018 began with a motto of promoting technology, scientific thinking and innovation, provided a platform for faculty and student researchers to showcase their technical skills. TechFest was a blend of innovation, brains and hard work which facilitated the technology for the future.

Hackathon

It will be a 24-hour offline event aimed at recognizing innovators from a young age. Hackathons urge students to combine all their knowledge to break down real business problems. Not only would this hackathon inspire students to pursue critical thinking, it would also allow them to think unconventionally . This hackathon would help them work on out of the box solutions and prepare them to apply these solutions to some real life problems.
