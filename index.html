About

BVEST 2018 began with a motto of promoting technology, scientific thinking and innovation, provided a platform for faculty and student researchers to showcase their technical skills. TechFest was a blend of innovation, brains and hard work which facilitated the technology for the future.

Events

Hackathon

It will be a 24-hour offline event aimed at recognizing innovators from a young age. Hackathons urge students to combine all their knowledge to break down real business problems.

This hackathon would help them work on out of the box solutions and prepare them to apply these solutions to some real life problems.

FAQs

What is the maximum team size?

Max team size will be 2 members per team.

What are timings?

Hackathon will begin at 10:00 and will end after 6-8 hours

What is the registration amount for hackathon?

We have kept registration amount to be ₹50.

How do I form or join a team?

We do encourage participants to start forming teams before the event. But you can collaborate in the event also.

What do I bring?

This is a very hands-on and grassroots event. Bring your laptops, mobile phones,to whatever inspires you but above all, your energy and your brain!

Are there any prizes for the hack categories?

We are able to provide cash and software prizes thanks to our sponsors!
